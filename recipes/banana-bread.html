Banana Bread

Description

Banana bread is a type of bread made with mashed bananas as the main ingredient. It is a moist and sweet bread that is typically served as a snack or dessert.

Ingredients

  • 3 ripe bananas, mashed
  • 1/3 cup melted butter
  • 1 teaspoon baking soda
  • Pinch of salt
  • 3/4 cup sugar
  • 1 large egg, beaten
  • 1 teaspoon vanilla extract
  • 1 1/2 cups all-purpose flour

Steps

  1. Preheat your oven to 350°F (175°C).
  2. In a mixing bowl, combine the mashed bananas and melted butter.
  3. Mix in the baking soda and salt.
  4. Stir in the sugar, beaten egg, and vanilla extract.
  5. Mix in the flour until just combined.
  6. Pour the batter into a greased 9x5 inch loaf pan.
  7. Bake for 50-60 minutes or until a toothpick inserted into the center of the bread comes out clean.
  8. Allow the bread to cool in the pan for 10 minutes before transferring it to a wire rack to cool completely.
